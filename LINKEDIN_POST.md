## Post

**Weekend project turned passion: Building the vet clinic management system I wish my vet had**

As a proud dog owner and animal lover, I've spent too many hours in Bulgarian vet clinics watching staff struggle with paper records, missed appointments, and chaotic billing. Last weekend I decided to fix it.

This is Vet Clinic - a modern management system built specifically for Bulgarian veterinary practices. 

**3 things I'm focusing on:**

1. **Zero paperwork chaos** - Everything from patient records to appointments in one clean dashboard. No more lost files or double-booking.

2. **Real-time clinic overview** - See revenue, pending appointments, vaccination schedules, and lab results at a glance. Know exactly what's happening in your clinic.

3. **Built for busy vets** - Quick actions for common tasks. Add patients, schedule appointments, or check invoices in seconds, not minutes.

**The problem it solves:** Bulgarian vet clinics lose 2-3 hours daily on administrative chaos. That's time not spent caring for animals. This system gives that time back.

This started as a weekend project but I'm putting my heart into it. As someone who loves animals, I want vets focusing on what they do best - healing our pets - not fighting with spreadsheets.

**Here's a quick look at the main dashboard interface.**

**Want to see it in action?** Visit the demo site at https://vetclinic.thecodevault.xyz and click "Log in with demo credentials" to see and feel how it works.

Currently testing with a few clinics. If you're a Bulgarian vet or know one who'd benefit from this, I'd love to hear what features would make their life easier.

Dog owners unite 🐕
